**By using Lulu, you agree to this Privacy Policy.** If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

## Appendix: Data Categories (Technical Reference)

For transparency, here is a detailed list of all data fields we collect:

### Baby Profile
- `id` (UUID)
- `userId` (Firebase Auth UID)
- `name` (string)
- `birthDate` (ISO 8601 string)
- `dueDate` (ISO 8601 string, optional)
- `isPremature` (boolean)
- `gender` ('male' | 'female' | 'other', optional)
- `birthWeightKg` (number, optional)
- `birthHeightCm` (number, optional)
- `birthHeadCircumferenceCm` (number, optional)
- `createdAt` (ISO 8601 string)
- `updatedAt` (ISO 8601 string)

### Activity Records
- `id` (UUID)
- `type` ('sleep' | 'feeding' | 'diaper' | 'play' | 'health')
- `timestamp` (ISO 8601 string)
- `durationMinutes` (number, optional)
- `endTime` (ISO 8601 string, optional)
- `amountMl` (number, for feeding)
- `feedingType` ('breast' | 'formula' | 'mixed', for feeding)
- `diaperType` ('wet' | 'dirty' | 'both', for diaper)
- `temperature` (number, for health)
- `weightKg` (number, for health growth records)
- `lengthCm` (number, for health growth records)
- `headCircumferenceCm` (number, for health growth records)
- `notes` (string, optional)
- `tags` (array of strings, for play activities)

This data is stored in Firebase Firestore under the path: `/users/{userId}/activities/{activityId}`
